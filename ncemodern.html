In 1988, Dr. Newman formed Competitive Edge, a small, woman owned training business. The mission of Competitive Edge is to provide organizations with the "best in the business" in communication, customer service, team building, management, supervisory and pre-retirement training. Dr. Newman was awarded Top Woman Business Enterprise Of 1994, by the Federal Transit Administration. Recently Dr. Newman has refined training seminars in Effective Informal and Formal Briefings Techniques, Report Writing, Customer Service and Pre-Retirement Planning. Focusing on the 21st Century, she is actively involved in innovative facilitative training designed to help organizations create solutions. Her tireless efforts to communicate employee and management needs generate win-win strategies at all organizational levels.

Dr. Newman has written and developed several communications and motivational workshops including the following programs: Improving Communications Skills; Team Building For Managers and Secretaries; Critical Thinking and Critical Reading; Techniques Of Supervision For Law Enforcement Agencies; Projecting Professionalism; Interviewing Techniques; Effective Briefing Techniques; Effective Briefings and Presentations; Goal Setting To Achieve Results; Assertiveness Training; Problem Solving Techniques For Managers; Writing, Proofreading and Editing For Secretaries; Performance Management; Effective Writing For Professionals; Fundamentals Of Memo Writing and Grammar; and Stress Management. This prospectus includes a current list of course outlines.

Competitive Edge has trained managers to build teams and communicate more effectively with their employees. Seminars encourage discussion, interactive communication strategies, role playing, and group discussions. Programs include personality, learning, listening, and communication styles; responsibilities of supervisors and managing change.

Contingent on individual client needs, new courses and seminars continue to be added. Should you require any specific changes, please contact Competitive Edge at (703) 533-3372 to arrange a consultation.

About Us

Dr. Newman received her undergraduate degree in English from Louisiana State University in Baton Rouge, La.; her Master of Arts degree in Speech Communication from Louisiana State University in Baton Rouge, La.; and her Doctorate of Communications from the University of Florida in Gainesville, Florida. She has held faculty appointments at Miami University of Ohio, College of Notre Dame, University of Maryland, and Bishop Dennis J. O'Connell High School.
